MentorPage

My mentor is Engineer Ibrahim Alkali, a distinguished figure in the field of Civil Engineering. Hailing from Maiduguri, Borno State, he has demonstrated exemplary dedication and expertise throughout his career. After completing his diploma at Ramat Polytechnic, he pursued further education at the University of Maiduguri, specializing in Civil Engineering. Engineer Alkali's contributions to the engineering community have not gone unnoticed, as he has been honored and awarded by prestigious institutions such as the Nigerian Institute of Civil Engineers and the Nigerian Society of Engineers. These accolades speak volumes about his proficiency and commitment to the field. One notable milestone in his career was his role as the project engineer of the third National FADAMA Development Project. His leadership and technical acumen were instrumental in the successful execution of this project, which had significant implications for agricultural development in Nigeria. Beyond his project engineering endeavors, Engineer Alkali has also served in governmental capacities. He was appointed as a Senior Special Advisor on Infrastructure to the Executive Governor of Borno State, where he provided invaluable insights and guidance on infrastructure development initiatives.

Currently, Engineer Alkali holds the esteemed position of Technical Special Advisor to the Federal Minister of Agriculture. In this role, he continues to leverage his expertise to drive forward policies and projects aimed at enhancing agricultural productivity and sustainability nationwide. Throughout his career journey, Engineer Ibrahim Alkali has remained a beacon of excellence and a source of inspiration for aspiring engineers. His unwavering dedication to his craft, coupled with his leadership qualities, exemplifies the very best of the engineering profession. As a mentee, I am privileged to learn from his wealth of experience and insights, aspiring to follow in his footsteps and make meaningful contributions to the field of Engineering.
